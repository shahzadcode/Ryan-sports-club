Yearly Activities

Indoor Sports Yearly Activities

Annual events, friendly matches, and coaching camps for Indoor Sports

  • Annual Sports Meet (Indoor): Badminton (Singles/Doubles), Table Tennis (Singles/Doubles), Squash (Singles/Doubles)
  • Friendly Matches (Indoor): Practice matches for Badminton, Table Tennis, Squash
  • Coaching Camps (Indoor): Skill development in Badminton, Table Tennis, Squash, including fitness and technique

Outdoor Sports Yearly Activities

Annual events, friendly matches, and coaching camps for Outdoor Sports

  • Annual Sports Meet (Outdoor): Football, Cricket, Tennis competitions and tournaments
  • Friendly Matches (Outdoor): Practice and fun matches for Football, Cricket, Tennis
  • Coaching Camps (Outdoor): Football (drills & tactics), Cricket (batting/bowling techniques), Tennis (serving & court coverage)

Recreational Yearly Activities

Wellness and fitness-focused activities throughout the year

  • Annual Sports Meet (Recreation): Yoga, Swimming, Gym activities with competitions and challenges
  • Friendly Matches (Recreation): Informal sessions and wellness challenges in Yoga, Swimming, and Gym activities
  • Coaching Camps (Recreation): Yoga (flexibility & strength), Swimming (stroke improvement), Gym (strength & endurance)
